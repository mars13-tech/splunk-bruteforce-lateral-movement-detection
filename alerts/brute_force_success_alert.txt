ALERT NAME: SSH_Brute_Force_Success

DESCRIPTION:
Detects successful SSH authentication following multiple failed login attempts.

SEVERITY:
Critical

TRIGGER CONDITION:
More than 5 failed login attempts followed by a successful login within 10 minutes.

DATA SOURCE:
Ubuntu Authentication Logs (/var/log/auth.log)

DETECTION QUERY:

index=main host="mars-VirtualBox"
("Failed password" OR "Accepted password")
| rex "from (?<src>\d+.\d+.\d+.\d+)"
| transaction src maxspan=10m
| search eventcount > 5 AND "Accepted password"

RECOMMENDED RESPONSE:

1. Verify user legitimacy.
2. Review account activity.
3. Reset compromised credentials.
4. Review subsequent system actions.
5. Investigate possible lateral movement.
6. Escalate incident if compromise is confirmed.
